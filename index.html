Tiered access levels

Choose the DealSight tier that matches your team.

From early-stage scouting to enterprise-level acquisition strategy, each tier is tuned to your scale, depth, and operational demands.

DealSight Access

Introductory tier for small acquisition teams.

  • Up to 3 users in a single Discord server
  • Core game intelligence and evaluation snapshot
  • Basic opportunity tracking and outreach status
  • Watchlist for 5 games with essential alerts
  • Private support included

Limitations: standard speed, short-term data retention, no advanced analytics.

DealSight Studio

Designed for active acquisition departments.

  • Up to 10 users across 3 Discord servers
  • AI-assisted stability indicators and momentum shifts
  • Multi-stage pipeline with assignments and tagging
  • Watchlist for 25 games with AI insights
  • Private support and workflow tooling

Limitations: medium-term retention, no executive-level intelligence suite.

DealSight Executive

Full intelligence suite for elite acquisition teams.

  • Unlimited users and Discord servers
  • Advanced AI inference and predictive insight tiers
  • Enterprise pipeline dashboards with lifecycle tracking
  • Unlimited watchlist with priority alerts
  • Priority support and DealSight Copilot

Limitations: none — maximum performance and retention.
